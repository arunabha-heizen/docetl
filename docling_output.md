## UNANIMOUS WRITTEN CONSENT OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS OF heizen

The undersigned, constituting all of the members of the Board of Directors (the "Board") of heizen, a DE corporation (the "Company"), acting pursuant to  the  applicable  provisions of the Delaware General Corporation Law and the Company's Bylaws, hereby adopt the following resolutions by unanimous written consent, effective as of December 1, 2025:

## RECITALS

WHEREAS , the Company previously issued a Simple Agreement for Future Equity (the "SAFE") to himanshu (the "Investor") on November 24, 2025 with a principal amount of $50,000.00;

WHEREAS , the Company desires to repurchase and cancel the SAFE from the Investor for a repurchase price of $25,000.00; and

WHEREAS ,  the  Board has determined that such repurchase is in the best interests of the Company and its stockholders.

## RESOLUTIONS

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT  RESOLVED , that the repurchase and cancellation  of  the  SAFE  issued  to  himanshu  for  a  repurchase  price  of $25,000.00 is hereby approved;

RESOLVED  FURTHER ,  that  the  officers  of  the  Company  are  hereby authorized  and  directed  to  execute  and  deliver  a  SAFE  Repurchase  and Cancellation  Agreement  (the  "Repurchase  Agreement")  with  the  Investor, substantially in the form presented to the Board;

RESOLVED  FURTHER ,  that  the  officers  of  the  Company  are  hereby authorized  and  directed  to  take  all  such  actions  and  execute  all  such documents as may be necessary or advisable to effectuate the repurchase and cancellation of the SAFE, including but not limited to the payment of the repurchase price and the cancellation of the SAFE; and

RESOLVED  FURTHER ,  that  any  actions  taken  by  the  officers  of  the Company prior to the date hereof in furtherance of the foregoing resolutions are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved in all respects.

IN  WITNESS WHEREOF, the undersigned have executed this Unanimous Written Consent as of the date first written above.
